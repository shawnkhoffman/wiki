Welcome to my wiki on Security.

Here are the current security practices, compliance standards, and frameworks that I have applied knowledge in.

Security Practices

AppSec

Finding, fixing, and preventing security issues within software applications.

InfoSec

The protection of information and network information systems.

Compliance Standards

  • SOC 2
  • PCI-DSS
  • CCPA
  • HIPAA
  • GDPR
  • CJIS
  • FISMA

SOC 2

System and Organization Controls.

A voluntary audit procedure and criteria established in the SOC framework set by the American Institute of CPAs (AICPA) that is geared for technology companies and third-party service providers that store consumer data in the cloud.

PCI-DSS

Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard.

An information security standard created by the organizations that handle major credit card brands to increase controls around cardholder data to reduce credit card fraud.

CCPA

California Consumer Privacy Act.

A state statute intended to enhance the privacy rights and consumer protections for the residents of California.

HIPAA

The Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act.

A U.S. federally-regulated set of standards to protect sensitive patient health information (PHI) from being disclosed without the patient's consent or knowledge.

GDPR

General Data Protection Regulation.

The toughest privacy and information security law in the world; a regulation in EU law on data protection and privacy in the European Union and the European Economic Area.

CJIS

The Criminal Justice Information Service Security Policy.

Required for anyone who has direct access to the U.S. Federal Bureau of Investigation's National Crime Information Center (NCIC) database, the Interstate Identification Index (III), state crime information databases, and U.S. law enforcement criminal investigation & location tracking systems.

FISMA

Federal Information Security Management Act.

The set of standards for security controls for the information systems of federal agencies identified in National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) Special Publication (SP) 800-53, “Recommended Security Controls for Federal Information Systems.”

Security Frameworks

Azure Security Architecture

Meeting industry standard security benchmarks using Microsoft's recommendations for cloud-native services on Microsoft Azure.

AWS Security Architecture

Meeting industry standard security benchmarks using Amazon's recommendations forr cloud-native services on AWS.

ISO 27001

The leading international standard for information security, published jointly by the International Organization for Standardization and the IEC.

CIS Controls

The Center for Internet Security's critical security controls designed such that that primarily automated means can be used to implement, enforce and monitor them.

FedRAMP

A U.S. federal government program that standardizes security assessment, authorization, and continuous monitoring for cloud services.

DISA STIGs

The Defense Information Systems Agency's configuration standards for the U.S. DoD's information assurance (IA) and IA-enabled devices/systems.
